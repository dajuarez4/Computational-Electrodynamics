3D FFT Poisson Viewer

An interactive Plotly view of the periodic 3D Poisson solve. The orange and blue surfaces mark positive and negative charge density, while the translucent surfaces show the equipotential structure recovered by the Fourier-space solver.

What this page shows

This is the static electrostatics counterpart to the FDTD pages: a volumetric solution of Poisson's equation in a periodic box, exported from C++ and rendered directly in the browser.

Open standalone HTML viewer Open visualization notebook Open C++ solver

Why it matters

The solver diagonalizes the Laplacian in Fourier space instead of iterating a relaxation scheme. That makes the static potential problem fast, clean, and ideal for comparing numerical electrostatics with the time-dependent Yee solvers.
