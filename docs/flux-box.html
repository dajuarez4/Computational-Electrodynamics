Flux Box

Drag and resize a Gaussian surface until its net enclosed charge matches the target flux. The field outside the box may look complicated, but Gauss's law only cares about what is inside.

How to play

  • Drag inside the glowing rectangle to move the Gaussian surface.
  • Drag a corner handle to resize it until the enclosed charge matches the target.
  • Press Check Flux when you think the surface is right.

Why this matters

For this game we use normalized units with ε0 = 1, so Gauss's law becomes ΦE = qenc. Outside charges can distort local field arrows, but they do not change the total flux through the surface.
